Understanding the Rise of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is reshaping industries, education, healthcare, and everyday life. But is it a blessing or a danger to society?

Positive Impacts of AI on Modern Society

  • AI improves healthcare by enabling early diagnosis and treatment recommendations.
  • It automates repetitive tasks, increasing productivity.
  • AI helps tackle climate change through smart predictions and monitoring.
  • Smart assistants like Siri and Alexa make life easier.

Challenges and Risks Associated with AI

  • AI can lead to job loss due to automation.
  • It may increase bias and discrimination if not properly trained.
  • AI surveillance raises privacy concerns.
  • AI weapons can be dangerous if misused.
